Ocean's Favourite Movies

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Two imprisoned men form an unlikely friendship over decades, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of kindness, hope, and quiet rebellion against injustice.

Inception (2010)

A skilled thief who invades people's dreams is tasked with the ultimate heist: planting an idea into a target's subconscious. But the deeper he goes, the harder it becomes to tell dream from reality.

Parasite (2019)

A poor family schemes their way into the lives of a wealthy household, but what starts as a clever plan spirals into a dark, unpredictable clash of class and secrets.
